What We Do

Our Services

Comprehensive inspection and compliance services for elevators and escalators throughout New York City.

01

Category 1 Inspections

Licensed annual inspections performed in full accordance with NYC elevator code requirements, ensuring your equipment stays compliant and operationally safe year over year.

02

Category 5 Testing

Comprehensive five-year full-load testing and inspection witnessing to verify system performance and ensure complete regulatory compliance across the inspection cycle.

03

Test Witnessing

Independent third-party witnessing for elevator and escalator testing, ensuring proper execution and thorough documentation of all required procedures with an unbiased eye.

04

Periodic Inspections

Routine inspections for elevators and escalators designed to identify maintenance issues early, support ongoing compliance standards, and prevent costly operational interruptions.
